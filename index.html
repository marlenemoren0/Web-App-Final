Why the 400m Is Unique

The 400m is often called the hardest sprint in track and field. It requires raw speed, pacing discipline, and the ability to push through extreme fatigue. Athletes complete one lap of a standard outdoor 400m athletics track while running in a designated lane. Indoors, athletes complete two laps of a standard indoor 200m track.
